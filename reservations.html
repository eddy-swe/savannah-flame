Common Questions

Reservations FAQ

Can I make a same-day reservation online?

Same-day reservations are not available through our online booking form. Please call us directly at +47 22 45 78 90 between our opening hours and we'll do our best to accommodate you. Walk-ins are also welcome subject to availability.

What is your cancellation policy?

We ask that you cancel at least 24 hours in advance so we can offer your table to another guest. For groups of 8 or more, we ask for 48 hours notice. Late cancellations or no-shows may result in a NOK 200 per-person fee for groups over 6.

Do you cater to dietary requirements?

Absolutely. Our menu features vegan and vegetarian dishes, and our kitchen can adapt many dishes for gluten-free, nut-free, and other requirements with advance notice. Please include details in your special requests, and our team will contact you to confirm.

Is there a dress code?

We describe our dress code as "smart casual." We want you to feel comfortable and special at the same time. While we don't enforce formal dress, we ask guests to avoid overly casual beachwear or sportswear in the main dining room.

Do you have parking?

There is no dedicated restaurant parking. We are located on Grünerløkka, which is well served by tram lines 11, 12, and 13 (stop: Birkelunden). Street parking is available nearby, and several public car parks are within a 5-minute walk.

Can we bring our own cake for a birthday?

Yes! We love celebrating with our guests. Please let us know in advance that you'll be bringing a cake so we can prepare a space for it. We do not charge a cakeage fee. Our pastry team can also create a bespoke celebration dessert if you'd prefer — enquire when booking.
