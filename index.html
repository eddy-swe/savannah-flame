Our Heritage

A Story Born From the Heart of Africa

"I wanted the world to taste Africa the way I grew up tasting it — vibrant, generous, alive."

Savannah Flame was founded in 2009 by Chef Amara Diallo with a singular dream: to honour the culinary diversity of an entire continent and share it with the world. From the smoky braais of Southern Africa to the aromatic stews of North Africa's medinas, every dish on our menu carries centuries of tradition.

What began as a modest dining room in Nairobi has grown into an internationally recognised destination — winner of three consecutive East Africa Restaurant Awards and a Condé Nast Traveller recommendation for five straight years.
