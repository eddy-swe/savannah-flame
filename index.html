Our Story

Born From the Heart of Africa

✦

Savannah Flame was born from a deep love for the vibrant cuisines of West, East, and Southern Africa — a desire to share the warmth, depth, and soul of these traditions with the world. Our founder, Chef Amara Diallo, left Dakar with little more than her grandmother's recipes and an unwavering belief that African food deserved a place at the world's finest tables.

Today, our kitchen blends ancestral techniques with contemporary presentation, creating dishes that are both deeply familiar and revelatory to those discovering them for the first time.
